🌽 Southern Corn Bread Recipe 🌽

Ingredients

  • 2 cups self-rising cornmeal mix
  • 2 eggs (or 1 extra large egg)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1/4 cup oil for cast iron skillet
  • 1 3/4 cups buttermilk or regular milk

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. If using cast iron, heat oil in skillet.
  3. Combine cornmeal mix, eggs, oil, and buttermilk.
  4. Mix until smooth.
  5. Pour batter into hot skillet.
  6. Transfer skillet to oven.
  7. Bake 25-30 minutes until golden brown.

Cooking Information

  • Prep Time: 30 Minutes
  • Cook Time: 45 Minutes
