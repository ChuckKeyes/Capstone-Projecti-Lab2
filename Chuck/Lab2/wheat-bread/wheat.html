🌾 Wheat Bread Recipe 🌾

Ingredients

  • 3 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1 cup bread flour
  • 1 packet active dry yeast
  • 1½ cups warm water
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. Combine warm water, honey, and yeast.
  2. Let stand 5 minutes until foamy.
  3. Add flour, oil, and salt.
  4. Mix and knead for 8-10 minutes.
  5. Place dough in a greased bowl and let rise 1 hour.
  6. Shape into a loaf and place in bread pan.
  7. Allow a second rise for 30 minutes.
  8. Bake at 375°F for 35-40 minutes.
