🍞 White Bread Recipe 🍞

Ingredients

  • 4 cups bread flour
  • 1 packet active dry yeast
  • 1½ cups warm water
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. Mix warm water, sugar, and yeast.
  2. Allow yeast to activate for 5 minutes.
  3. Add flour, butter, and salt.
  4. Knead until smooth.
  5. Allow dough to rise for 1 hour.
  6. Place in loaf pan.
  7. Bake at 375°F for 30-35 minutes.
